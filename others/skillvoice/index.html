Note:

The transcripts were created by an AI that listens to the audios and transcribes them, so expect a bunch of mistakes – even onomatopoeias might end up as entire sentences that make zero sense.

Another issue is with words that sound similar, like "Fox" and "Fuc*", which might turn into phrases that even Empress Cygnus wouldn’t want to hear!

For Hayato and Kanna, the AI was based on the original language of the classes and their voice lines, so there could be major issues with the intended meaning – after all, snails don’t speak Japanese.

If you spot any problems with the transcripts, drop an email at extractwithbob@gmail.com.
